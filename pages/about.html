About Me

I am a researcher in Multi-Agent Multi-Robot Systems and UAVs, with a strong focus on Autonomous Systems, Federated Learning, Swarm Learning, Evolutionary Algorithms, Genetic Programming, and Bio-inspired Artificial Intelligence. My work explores decentralized intelligence, adaptive coordination, and life-long learning in robotic systems inspired by collective behaviours observed in nature. I have co-authored three research papers on decentralized learning strategies in multi-robot environments.
